Desertification

Desertification turns healthy land into dry, barren deserts, making it hard for plants, animals, and people to live or grow food there.

See more

Deforestation

Deforestation destroys animal homes, making it hard for wildlife to survive and causes more carbon dioxide to stay in the air, which worsens climate change.

See more

Air polution

Air pollution can damage plants and trees by blocking sunlight and harming their leaves, making it harder for them to grow.
