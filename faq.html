Why we came up to this project and how Qusetions and Ansers

How did you come up with the idea for your piece?
We started by thinking about some of the biggest environmental issues affecting the planet today, and we chose the SDGs no15 “Life one Land” So we searched some problems happening on the earth right now and decided to make a project about Deforestation, desertification, and air pollution. Those problems stood out because personally, I think that these problems are rapidly getting worse everyday.
What inspired your work and creative process? What were the key factors that influenced the concept of this project?
Our work was inspired by a blend of experiences in other countries and a desire to stop the problems from environmental damage. The creative process was because of the experiences when I was a child allowing us to find things we needed to stop.
Why did you choose to make a video?
We choose to make a video because we want to show how the environmental damage could have an effect in many ways. The video is showing how the damage occurs and how it affects the environment and more.

What problem or need led us to develop this project?
Some problems that we faced were when we made the website. I had hard times especially when I put in new things I didn’t know like getting a video, photo, and maps. Luckily my friend helped to do my website. That led us to make a solid project.
What motivated us to pursue this idea?
We were motivated to pursue this idea because we identified desertification, deforestation, and air pollution that weren't being effectively addressed. My awareness of nature from America, combined with research made me motivated to do this sort of project. This idea could have and saw a real opportunity to create value and drive change.
What were the key factors that influenced the concept of this project?
We were thinking about how we could raise awareness in a simple but impactful way. Therefore, we decided to influence people by making a website and a video to show and tell how threatening those problems are and the ways to stop them.
